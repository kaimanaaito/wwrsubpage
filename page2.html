-The Great River- Facility Information

Experience the thrill of white water rafting on the Salmon River and other exciting rivers!
From the rugged beauty of Idaho's wilderness to the breathtaking views of the Grand Canyon, our rafting adventures offer unforgettable experiences for thrill-seekers.

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Nestled deep in the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return winds the Salmon river. The Salmon was given the nickname long ago as the River of No Return by the people living near it because of the number of rapids.

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

Winding through canyons in central Utah, the Green River flows towards the mighty Colorado River. This 84-mile trip through some of the most desolate land offers stunning views, great rapids, and a history-rich adventure.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

Experience world-class whitewater rafting through the Grand Canyon! With exhilarating rapids and breathtaking scenery, this trip offers one of the most unforgettable adventures in the world.
